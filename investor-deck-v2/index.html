Executive Summary GymGaze
02 / Executive Summary
A new media network. Built and ready to deploy.

GymGaze is a digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising network built specifically for gyms. We install screens in high-traffic zones, sell ad slots to SA brands and agencies on a CPM model (R65–R85 per 1 000 impressions), and share revenue with the gym partner — 70% to GymGaze, 30% to the venue.

The first partnership is signed. Edge Fitness — one of South Africa's leading gym chains — has committed 14 venues across three provinces for a 3-year term. 94 screens are ready to deploy. The contract is active.

The ask is R 410 000 — purely a hardware and installation capital round. The platform (Cuecast) is already built and running. No software costs, no licensing, no third-party dependency. This round purchases the screens and gets them installed.

The return: Dividends begin within 12 months of deployment (20% of net profit, bi-annually). When GymGaze reaches R 1M annual revenue — projected 12–18 months post-deployment — Mlu may buy back your equity at R 820 000. That is 2× your capital in cash.

Why Now?

Partner signed. Edge Fitness contract is executed. No prospecting risk — the venue network is secured.

Platform built. Cuecast is live and operational. Zero software development cost in this round.

One round closes the gap. R 410 000 buys the hardware. Nothing else stands between pre-revenue and first ad live.

Revenue Model GymGaze
08 / Revenue Model
CPM-Based Ad Sales. 70/30 Revenue Split.

Advertisers pay per thousand impressions (CPM). Each screen loops ads continuously throughout operating hours. Standard 7-second slots and premium 15-second slots are sold at different rates, depending on campaign requirements.

Slot TypeDurationCPM Rate
Standard7 secondsR 65
Premium15 secondsR 85

16 ad slots per screen per loop (8×7s + 8×15s)

Revenue Split
70% — GymGaze
30% Edge

Rental fees: R 1 300–R 2 500/venue/month, activating at ≥35% occupancy floor.

Risk & Mitigations GymGaze
14 / Risk & Mitigations
We've thought about what could go wrong.
Risk
Mitigation
Slow advertiser ramp-up
Edge Fitness contract guarantees venue access for 3 years. GymGaze actively books advertisers — no dependency on venue to sell.
Screen hardware failure
GymGaze-warrantied equipment, remote monitoring via Cuecast health alerts, on-site repair within 48 hours.
Agency payment delays
Dividends paid bi-annually specifically to account for 45–60 day agency terms. No month-to-month cash flow risk to investor.
Venue exits contract early
3-year term with 6-month notice. GymGaze retains screens (GymGaze-owned hardware) and can redeploy to new venues.
Advertiser category conflicts
Edge Fitness provides approved/excluded advertiser category list. GymGaze enforces content moderation on all creative.

The biggest risk is slow sell-through. The mitigation is the 35% occupancy floor — rental only activates when we've proven commercial momentum.
