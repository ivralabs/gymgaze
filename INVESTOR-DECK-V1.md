## Slide 2 — The Problem

### Gyms are packed with people. Nobody is monetising that.

- South Africa has **1 000+ gyms** and **3 million+ members** — all captive, all spending time
- Members visit **3–5× per week**, staying **45–90 minutes** per session — a dwell time no billboard can match
- Brands want to reach **health-conscious, LSM 6–10 consumers** — but no direct advertising channel exists inside gyms
- Traditional OOH (billboards, transit) catches people in passing. **Gym screens catch people standing still.**

> The audience is already there. The screens aren't.

---

## Slide 3 — The Solution

### GymGaze puts digital advertising screens inside gyms — and handles everything.

- **Install** digital screens in high-traffic gym zones (entrance, cardio floor, free weights)
- **Sell** ad slots to brands and agencies at CPM pricing — R65–R85 per 1 000 impressions
- **Manage** all content, scheduling, and campaign reporting via the Cuecast platform
- **Gym partner earns 30% of revenue** — passive income, zero effort, no capital outlay

> Gyms get a new income stream. Brands get a new channel. GymGaze runs it all.

## Slide 6 — The Business Model

### CPM ad sales. Simple. Scalable.

- **Standard slot (7s):** R65 CPM
- **Premium slot (15s):** R85 CPM
- **16 ad slots per screen per loop** (8 × 7s + 8 × 15s)
- Revenue split: **70% GymGaze / 30% venue partner**
- Additional revenue stream: **widget sponsorships** — brands sponsor the news, sports, and weather widgets displayed on-screen between ads

> The inventory is our screens. The product is eyeballs. The math works.

## Notes for Designer

### Slide 1 — Cover
- **Background:** Deep charcoal (`#1A1A1A`) or near-black with a subtle gym texture overlay (dark rubber flooring / weights blurred in background)
- **Logo:** GymGaze wordmark, large, centred — white or electric green
- **Tagline:** Italic, muted white, smaller beneath the logo
- **Founder name + date:** Bottom right, small, white
- **Emphasis:** Tagline should feel like a headline — bold colour accent on "advertising real estate"

### Slide 2 — The Problem
- **Background:** Dark grey (`#212121`)
- **Key visual:** Split image — busy gym floor left, empty billboard right — visual contrast of where people are vs. where advertising lives
- **Emphasis:** Pull stat to large display — *"45–90 minutes per visit"* or *"3–5× per week"* — make the dwell time visceral
- **Callout box:** The closing quote in a highlighted block, offset from bullets

### Slide 3 — The Solution
- **Background:** Slightly lighter grey (`#2A2A2A`) — differentiate from Problem slide
- **Key visual:** Mockup of a GymGaze screen installed on a gym wall — product in context
- **Layout:** 4-point icon row (Install / Sell / Manage / Earn) with short labels beneath each
- **Emphasis:** "30% revenue" in accent colour (electric green or brand orange)

### Slide 4 — The Opportunity
- **Background:** Brand colour (deep green `#0D3B2E` or dark navy) — signal a gear shift
- **Key visual:** South Africa map shape, lightly etched/watermark — localises the opportunity
- **Emphasis:** "R4.5 billion" as a hero number — 72pt+ — let it dominate the slide
- **"First mover"** statement in a distinct callout box at bottom

### Slide 5 — The Product
- **Background:** Dark (`#1E1E1E`)
- **Key visual:** Cuecast dashboard screenshot (or clean mockup) — show the product exists
- **Layout:** Left — product screenshot. Right — 4 bullet points.
- **Emphasis:** "Built. Running. Ready to deploy." as a bold header — proof, not promise

### Slide 6 — The Business Model
- **Background:** Dark charcoal
- **Key visual:** Simple diagram — Screen → Impressions → CPM → Revenue → Split (70/30)
- **Layout:** Flow diagram beats bullet points here — show the money moving
- **Emphasis:** Highlight R65 and R85 CPM rates prominently; widget sponsorship as an inset "bonus" stat

### Slide 7 — Revenue Projections

- **Footer note:** Phase 2 expansion in small print below table
